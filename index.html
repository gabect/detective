Digital Detectives: Case File

Case 07: The Viral School Cancellation Post

Mission Brief

You are a digital detective team investigating a viral post shared by fictional student Jordan Rivera. The post claims that Ridgeway Middle School is canceled tomorrow. Your job is to examine evidence across several sources and decide what really happened.

  • Read each source carefully.
  • Look for clues about trustworthiness, timing, and verification.
  • Record your notes on the Evidence Board.

Evidence Sources

1) Social Profile 2) Message Thread 3) Email Inbox 4) Search Results 5) Evidence Board

Final Question

What really happened?

Use at least three clues from different pages to support your conclusion.
