Case 07 Facilitation Guide

Answer Key

Jordan shared an unverified rumor that school was canceled. Official sources (school office email, district alerts, and official website) all showed that school and testing were still on.

Intended Clues

  • Message thread: Jordan admits posting before verifying.
  • Email inbox: official school message says testing is still scheduled.
  • Search results: official site and district alerts confirm school is open.
  • Social comments: peers ask for official proof, signaling doubt.
  • Suspicious newsletter uses dramatic language and no confirmation.

Suggested Discussion Questions

  1. Which clues were facts, and which were opinions or rumors?
  2. Why do viral posts spread faster than verified information?
  3. What are two ways to verify school announcements?
  4. How can sharing false info affect classmates and families?
